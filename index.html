About LMDG Logistics

LMDG Logistics is a specialized manpower partner for logistics operations across warehouses, industrial plants, ports, and infrastructure projects. We focus exclusively on material handling equipment operators, logistics workforce, and driver solutions.

Safety and reliability are at the core of our operations. Our operators are trained and compliant with client safety norms, ensuring smooth onsite execution and minimal downtime.

We build long-term partnerships with clients to support critical operations, offering flexible engagement models tailored to project needs and timelines.
